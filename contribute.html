Consider

Contributions are not expected to be complete.

Only sincere.

Consider

The goal is not to promote a solution.

It is to clarify a shared problem space.

Dead End

Contributions that push a fixed outcome tend to close the space.

Direction

Contributions that:

– clarify meaning
– make intention visible
– connect ideas across contexts
– reveal structure

help expand the space.

Consider

Attribution is preserved where possible.

Not as ownership— but as continuity of reasoning.
